# BDO Wizard Teleport Audit

Teleport I-III is a Wizard/Witch Main skill. The catalog levels are 13, 38, and 51 with SP costs
4, 9, and 16. Rank cooldowns are 9, 8, and 7 seconds. Current BDO uses 200 Stamina for every rank,
grants Invincible during the skill, and grants Movement Speed +10% for 10 seconds.

BRPG scales the source Stamina cost by 10:1 to 20 because its Breath pool starts near 105 rather
than BDO's much larger Stamina scale. Cooldowns remain exact at 180/160/140 ticks. Distance 8 blocks,
cast 4 ticks, recovery 4 ticks, and the cursor-selection timeout are provisional feel values.
